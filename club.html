RPI Drone Club

Who Are We?

We are a brand new club at RPI that wants to embody a seriously cool drone industry, and provide its members with the relevant technical skills to take advantage of it. Our club is officially chartered with the Academy of Model Aeronautics (AMA), and a participating member of the Collegiate Drone Racing Association.

"For the battle against gravity!"
